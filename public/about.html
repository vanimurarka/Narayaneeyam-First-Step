About the Website

This is a venture to give the Sanskrit word meanings in the order of the Shlokas, and not in अन्वय form. For this the two references are:
1) Shriman Narayaneeyam by Shri S. N. Sastri, published by CCMT, Mumbai
2) Narayaneeyam - translated by Swami Tapasyananda, published by Sri Ramakrishna Math, Chennai

The need for this was voiced by a ladies study group, where Late Shri C. S. Nair was teaching Narayaneeyam. I invite readers to point out any mistakes, shortcomings or suggestions.

With deep humbleness and high gratitude, I dedicate this small endeavour to Late Shri N. S. Venkatakrishnan, who introduced me to this great stotra. I also extend my homage to Late Shri C. S. Nair, who, with great confidence entrusted this work to me. My salutations to both of them and my parents.
